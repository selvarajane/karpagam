How to make the monthly advance payment?

Advance payments should reach KARPAGAM JEWELLERS before the 10th of every month. Payments can be made by Cash / UPI / Debit cards / Credit cards. At-par cheques, Outstation cheques will not be accepted. As per Government of India regulations, in case of more than â‚¹1,99,000, PAN is mandatory. In case of cheque dishonour, bank charges will be borne by the customers. The payment can be made at KARPAGAM JEWELLERS showroom or by downloading the KARPAGAM JEWELLERS mobile app.

Will there be GST?

Yes, GST and any other Government levies will be borne by the customer at the time of delivery.

How do I know the amount / weight accumulated?

This will be updated in the customer receipt book on a monthly basis at the time of making the advance payment.

Can I make all the monthly payments in advance?

The monthly payments cannot be carried over, or paid in advance.

Can I buy Gold Coins?

Yes, you can purchase gold coins with No Wastage (VA) and making charges.

Is the monthly advance amount fixed or variable?

The monthly advance amount is fixed.

Will cash be refunded?

Cash will not be refunded under any circumstance, as per Government regulations.
