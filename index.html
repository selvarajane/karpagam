Who we are

Experience the joy of
jewelry made with heart,

Incorporated in the year 1965 at Tiruvannamalai city, "Karpagam Jewellery" is crafting some of the finest gold and diamond jewellery. Being the Best Jewellers of Tiruvannamalai brings to you artists that have experience in crafting glorious and magnificent ornaments that will suit each and everyone's taste. Our optimum range of jewellery comprises Gold Earring, Diamond Earring, and Rose Gold jewellery.

Known for our exclusive designs, exceptional craftsmanship, and adherence to international quality standards, we offer an unmatched collection for both men and women. With decades of expertise and a passion for perfection, we continue to serve our valued customers with elegance, trust, and excellence.
